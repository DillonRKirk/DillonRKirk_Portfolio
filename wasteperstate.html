Waste per State Resident (2022)

This choropleth map depicts the amount of waste (in tons) normalized by state population. I chose a multi-hued seven classed choropleth map pursuant to Color Brewer recommendations. Darker shades represent more waste per state resident. The map employs leader lines to draw the map reader's attention to Indiana and New York. In later maps, I employed a macro-micro storytelling technique to highlight differences in landfill operations.
